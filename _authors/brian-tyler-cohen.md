Brian Tyler Cohen is an American author, progressive podcast host, YouTuber, and political commentator. He is the host of the political podcast No Lie with Brian Tyler Cohen and manages a YouTube channel where he reports on politics, live-streams events such as debates and election results, and interviews political figures.

As of February 2026, his YouTube channel has garnered more than 5.01 million subscribers and over 4.79 billion views.
