Charles Duhigg is an American journalist and non-fiction author. Formerly a reporter for The New York Times, he currently writes for The New Yorker Magazine. In 2013, he received the Pulitzer Prize for Explanatory Reporting for a series of ten articles examining the business practices of Apple and other technology companies.

Duhigg is the author of three books: The Power of Habit: Why We Do What We Do in Life and Business, Smarter Faster Better, and Supercommunicators: How to Unlock the Secret Language of Connection.
