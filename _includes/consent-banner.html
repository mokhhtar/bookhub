{%- comment -%} Two-tier cookie consent for the GA4 setup in _layouts/default.html, built to the GDPR / ePrivacy pattern: * Two categories only, because that is all the site actually has — Necessary (theme, reading list, rate-limit id, Firebase session; always on, exempt from consent) and Analytics (GA4; opt-in). * "Accept all" and "Necessary only" carry equal visual weight — reject is as easy as accept, no dark pattern, no cookie wall. * Prior consent: GA is never loaded until "Accept all" (see the head). * Withdrawal is as easy as consent: a "Cookie preferences" link in the footer re-opens this banner any time (lithecaOpenConsent). * The choice is recorded with a timestamp and policy version so it can expire (12 months) or be re-asked when the policy changes. {%- endcomment -%}
